Morotsi Mining: Building a Legacy of Excellence

Morotsi Mining (PTY) Ltd. isn't just a river sand mining contractor; we're builders, innovators, and partners. Established with a vision to redefine the mining landscape in Botswana, we bring a unique blend of experience, cutting-edge techniques, and an unwavering commitment to our clients and the communities we serve.

Our foundation rests on a deep understanding of the complexities of the mining industry. We recognize that each project presents unique challenges and opportunities. That's why we take a collaborative, client-focused approach, working closely with our partners to develop tailored solutions that maximize efficiency, safety, and sustainability.

Our Values: The Pillars of Our Success

  • Innovation: We constantly seek new and better ways to approach mining challenges, embracing technology and creative thinking to drive progress.
  • Integrity: We conduct our business with the highest ethical standards, earning the trust of our clients, partners, and communities.
  • Excellence: We strive for excellence in everything we do, from project execution to client service, delivering results that exceed expectations.
  • Partnership: We believe in building strong, collaborative relationships with our clients, working as a team to achieve shared goals.
  • Sustainability: We are committed to responsible mining practices that minimize environmental impact and contribute to the long-term well-being of the communities where we operate.

At Morotsi Mining, we're not just extracting resources; we're building a legacy of excellence, innovation, and sustainable development in the Botswana mining sector. We invite you to join us on this journey.

Our Visionary Founder & CEO: Mino Morotsi

Mino Morotsi

Founder & Chief Executive Officer

mino.morotsi@morotsimining.com

LinkedIn Profile

Morotsi Mining stands as a profound testament to the unwavering vision, relentless resilience, and sheer grit of our founder, Mino Morotsi. Her journey into the often-grueling world of mining was not paved with formal qualifications in the sector, but rather forged through countless hours of sweat, profound tears, and an unyielding spirit in an adjacent, yet equally demanding, industry.

Mino's deep familiarity with large-scale projects, heavy machinery, logistics, and robust project management stems from her successful ownership and operation of Nthabie Construction. Though a small-to-medium enterprise, Nthabie Construction has built a strong reputation as a renowned local business, having significantly contributed to the country's essential infrastructure development over the years. This hands-on experience has provided her with an invaluable understanding of industrial operations, resource management, and the complexities of large-scale ground-breaking endeavors.

Inspired by the remarkable success and deep-rooted knowledge of her uncle, a highly esteemed veteran who has carved out a significant niche in the mining sector, Mino didn't just see a new business venture; she envisioned a powerful platform for transformative impact. Her decision to embark on this new journey with Morotsi Mining is driven by a profound personal mission: to actively encourage and pave the way for other aspiring entrepreneurs, particularly black, talented women, to enter and thrive in industries traditionally dominated by others.

Mino believes that true progress lies in diversifying leadership and ownership within critical sectors. Her foray into mining is a bold declaration of her commitment to demonstrating that success is not solely defined by academic accolades in a specific field, but by vision, dedication, practical experience, and an unwavering commitment to breaking barriers. Morotsi Mining is more than a business; it's a beacon of possibility, built on her tireless work and a passionate belief in inclusive economic empowerment.

Background: Founder & CEO of Nthabie Construction, extensive experience in infrastructure development and project management.
